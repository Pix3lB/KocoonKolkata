Empowering Women through Education

Creating opportunities for middle-aged women to learn and thrive in the real world.

Our Mission

We focus on educating middle-aged women, enhancing their skills to face real-world challenges confidently.

Our Goals

  • Provide financial literacy to manage personal finances.
  • Promote digital literacy to help navigate the modern world.
  • Raise awareness about legal rights and health issues.

Our Services

  • Workshops on financial management.
  • Classes for basic and advanced computer skills.
  • Legal rights and self-defense awareness programs.
